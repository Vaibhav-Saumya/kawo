Adopt an animal

There are millions of stray and homeless animals in India. If every individual were to adopt one stray/homeless animal, the number of those on the streets will become negligible.

Donate an animal

Donate for cause

About Us

Welcome to
KAWO

Kalyan Animal Welfare Organisation is a non-profit charity organization dedicated to supporting efficient and effective animal protection organizations in India. We strive to put our donor's generous contributions to work where they are needed the most. KAWO develops close relationships with India's animal welfare NGOs (non-government organizations). This ensures that all donations made to save India's animals are transparent and do the most good.
